Respect and inclusion

  • Treat other members with respect regardless of rank, background or beliefs.
  • No harassment, hate speech, or targeted abuse.
  • Use English in public HQ channels whenever possible for clarity.

Behavioural rules

  • No griefing, trolling, or intentionally disruptive behaviour in NSC rooms.
  • Double-jobbing and badges associated with disallowed groups are prohibited.
  • Follow trainer guidance during training sessions; warnings and penalties apply for repeated offences.
