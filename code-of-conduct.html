NSC Handbook — Code of Conduct

This handbook outlines the rules, responsibilities, and procedures for NSC members. Click a section title to expand details.

Show respect to everyone, regardless of rank, gender, religion, nationality, disability, or political affiliation.

Do not flood, spam, or troll in base unless permitted by the Foundation Team under special consideration.

English is the recommended mode of communication in HQ. Other languages are allowed if respectful to the agency’s members and visitors.

Only work at the NSC. Double jobbing will lead to immediate termination with proper documentation.

Do not ask or hint for a promotion, pay, room rights, badge admin, portal admin, Discord admin, etc., unless permitted by the Foundation Team.

Ignore and report trolls and raiders to HR and above.

Association with any Mafia is prohibited. Violations will result in proper disciplinary actions.

Any risk of exposure of personal data will be terminated (e.g., harassment, blackmail).

When in doubt or facing a problem, ask an HR+ member for assistance. “Asking is caring.”

No excessive swearing/cursing in base. Proper decorum must be observed.

(Removed numbering inconsistency; original Section 1.1 skipped 11.)

Do not self-promote or self-pay without prior permission.

Correct badge and motto must always be observed in base.

Always follow the Habbo Way and Discord’s Terms and Conditions.

Do not troll other agencies or rooms that may affect the agency’s reputation.

Do not abuse the power given to you as Office Supervisor+ or divisions.

Office Supervisor+ are accountable for conflicts in base. Problems should be solved with precise due process; escalate to Internal Affairs if necessary.

For conflicts beyond your reach, provide evidence (screenshot or video with details).

Promotions are held after 15 minutes of OT in base and filling a station for 15 minutes. Present promotions to the promoter.

Favoritism is not tolerated; all members are considered equal with due respect.

  • FTF – Fill the Front: Welcome returning members, visitors, and recruits.
  • FTB – Fill the Back: Sit in the AFK area when idle or away.
  • FTS – Fill the Security: Work at Security Station to check member status and allow entry.
  • FTT – Fill the Training: Send recruits to the training area and train them.
  • BTB – Back to Base: Return to base immediately.
  • CTH – Clear the Hall: Clear the hall and return to a station or FTB area.
  • ATT – Attention: Stand in front of a member, wave, and say “Yes, Ma’am/Sir.”
  • AE – At Ease: Return to your previous activity.

Badges

Official NSC badges are required with the correct rank. Special Visitors (SV) and VIPs must wear theirs for identification.

Motto

Must reflect the correct rank at all times.

Official Dress Code

Members may wear anything except:

  • Attire promoting raid groups (e.g., mafia)
  • Offensive attire promoting racial stereotypes
  • Revealing or sexually inappropriate clothing

Promotions are given only by Office Supervisor+ members.

Pay Sessions: Six times daily at 2AM, 6AM, 10AM, 2PM, 6PM, 10PM GMT+8.

Requirements to qualify for pay:

  • At least 30 minutes online
  • Be at base to fill a station 15 mins before session
  • Join pay session via portal within 5 minutes after opening
  • If disconnected, log the disconnection on Discord’s #disconnection-logs

Bonus Points: Stay 15 mins after pay = 1 bonus point (max 3 bonus points per day). Earn by doing training or tours.

Weekly Pay Bonus, quotas, and claim rules are maintained by the Foundation. See internal Finance documentation for details.

RR and BA are privileges granted by the Foundation, not rights. Abuse will result in disciplinary action. RR/BA can never be bought.

Approved rank sellers must report transactions to the Finance and Audit Overseer for documentation.

Standard Division: Recruit new members.

Security Division: Maintain order and protect agency assets by enforcing security protocols and monitoring activity.

Education Division: Educate and mentor new agents, ensuring they are equipped with the skills and mindset for success.

Office Supervisors Division: Evaluate agent performance and promote qualified individuals to Trainer rank with fairness and insight.

Overseers Division: Promote Standard → Supervisor; supervise departmental operations and ensure compliance.

Management Officers Division: Promote Standard → Overseers; conduct tours and onboarding sessions.

Public Relations Division: Represent the agency externally; manage communications.

Consultancy Division: Provide expert guidance and resolve inquiries.

Government Affairs Division: Coordinate policies and uphold agency governance.

Professional Staff, Senior Executive, Leadership Council, Prestigious Council, Elites: Various leadership and advisory responsibilities as described in internal documentation.

Monthly meeting for Division Heads. Non-iC members may give input after deliberation. ICs/Special Units must hold one internal meeting prior to monthly meeting. Failure to meet quotas may result in a meeting with Advisory Team/Foundation.

Pay Hosts: Handle paytimes; have badge & Discord tag; cannot be deprived of pay if eligible; late hosts must notify director.

Pay Managers: Distribute funds; only they may pay; bribes or harassment result in removal.

Strike System:

  1. 1st Strike: Verbal Warning
  2. 2nd Strike: First Warning logged
  3. 3rd Strike: Second Warning + 1 rank demotion
  4. 4th Strike: Third Warning + 1 division demotion

Severe repetitive offenses = Immediate Termination. National Punishment (NP), Ban on Sight (BoS), Do Not Hire (DNH) may be applied for serious violations.

Reinstatement and Appeal: Conditions vary; may lose rank. Maximum of 3 reinstatements allowed. Appeals handled by Founders, Advisory Team, 5iCs. No bribery or favoritism allowed in appeals.

NSC does not currently take alliances. Contact Director of External Affairs or Foundation for proposals.

Transfer Policy: Transfers by Department of Labor and Employment (DoLE) only. Must meet member requirements and pass trial training.

Portal Usage: Allowed: Screenshot portal rank for proof, report glitches, record incidents for Internal Affairs. Not allowed: Disclose infractions without IA knowledge, pay themselves outside of pay sessions.

Veterans’ Committee: For retired members; 3-month active service required. Perks: SV, VIP, Veteran Badge, website recognition. Revocation possible for misconduct.

LOA (Leave of Absence): iC: >7 days → LOA required. Non-iC: >14 days → LOA required. LOA up to 45 days; approval by 1iC+. Emergency LOA allowed with direct approval.

Discord is mandatory for communication. Tagging system must be used for official business. Members must abide by Discord Terms of Service. Harassment, exploitation, blackmail, or personal gain is prohibited.

Inactive or terminated members are removed from roles; severe cases banned. English is required for communication to prevent confusion. NSC is not liable for missed announcements if member fails to join Discord.

All members must react to Internal Affairs daily announcements regarding the Code of Conduct. Failure to react may result in disciplinary action.

Special Visitors, Veterans, Allies: Discord access is a privilege. Misconduct may result in removal of Discord access or badges. Leaks of sensitive info = removal from Discord and badges.

Intent to harm or disrupt the community must be reported to responsible personnel instead of taking unauthorized action.
