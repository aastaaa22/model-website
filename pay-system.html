DEPT OF FINANCE POLICY

Standard Pay System — Read carefully: eligibility rules, pay distribution, bonus and pay-bonus quotas.

Standard Pay System

Pay sessions take place four (4) times a day at 2:00 AM, 6:00 AM, 2:00 PM, and 6:00 PM (GMT+8).

To qualify for pay, members must have at least 30 minutes of online time and be present in base to fill a station 15 minutes before the session opens. For example: if a pay session opens at 2:00 PM, members should be online by 1:30 PM and at base to fill a station on or before 1:45 PM.

To receive pay:

  1. Members must join the pay session via the NSC Portal within five (5) minutes after the payer opens the session.
  2. After the session closes the payer will check each member's eligibility (online time and station fill) one-by-one. Members must have filled a station for 30 minutes to avoid pay ban.
  3. If a member disconnects before, during or after pay they must immediately notify the payer and log the disconnection in Discord’s #disconnection-logs with proper format — tag the payer and any witnesses for vouch.

Note: If a non-agent member fails to join the pay session within the allotted time, their pay will be voided for that session.

Pay distribution (Standard)

Pay is distributed per rank. (1 point = 1 credit)

Standard Division

Clerk: 1 point

Security to Education

2 points

Office Supervisor to Management Officers

3 points

Public Relations to Professional Staff

4 points

Senior Executives to Elite

5 points

iC Divisions (5iC to 1iC)

6 points

Payers must ensure all participants meet requirements before approving pay. Violations may result in removal from Department of Finance, additional disciplinary measures, or permanent expulsion from NSC.

Special Notice

Promotions, training and tours are available during pay time except paylines: paylines are expected to line up to be included in the list of people to pay at the Portal.

Bonus Points

Every member is eligible to earn up to 6 bonus points per day, resetting every 24 hours. Members request bonus points in the Portal with included evidence (training / tour):

  • Up to 3 points from tours
  • Up to 3 points from trainings

Pay Bonus

Starting from Education+ division, members may be eligible for Pay Bonus if they meet quotas by the end of the week (Sunday 3:00 AM to Friday 11:59 PM GMT+8).

Payers, Advisory Boards and Founders can accept Pay Bonus requests until every Friday 11:59 PM GMT+8. Any member finishing their quota must not receive verbal warnings/strikes/punishment (including Pay Ban) or they will be voided from the Pay Bonus.

Quotas (summary)

  • Education Division: 3 trainings; Promoted 3 times
  • Office Supervisor to Overseer: Promote others 8 times; Promoted 4 times
  • Management → Elites: choose one option below:
    • Tour: 2 times; Promote others: 12 times
    • Train: 2 times; Promote others: 12 times
    • Train: 1 time; Tour: 1 time; Promote others: 10 times
    • Promoted: 4 times; Promote others: 15 times

If quotas are met, members are entitled to a 20c Pay Bonus every Saturday. Members have 24 hours (Saturday 2:00 AM to Sunday 2:00 AM GMT+8) to claim their Pay Bonus from available Payers, ABs, or Founders. Unclaimed bonuses will be removed automatically.
