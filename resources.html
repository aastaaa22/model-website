CTH – Clear The Halls – Go to a station or FTB; don’t crowd the hallway.

The Education Division is responsible for onboarding and educating new recruits within the National Security Council.
Educators conduct structured sessions that introduce agency protocols, rank systems, workstations, and expectations.
They ensure every recruit is equipped with the knowledge and confidence to serve NSC with professionalism and pride.
Educators also log sessions through the NSC Portal and uphold training standards.
As frontline educators, they play a vital role in shaping the future of NSC.
In this training, we will be discussing your new role, activating a portal and how to apply on LOA.
