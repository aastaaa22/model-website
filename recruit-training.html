We will now begin the Basic Commands you will need to know.
Please do not perform the commands as I go through it with you.
You will be given a chance to practice at the later stage.
ATT – Attention - Stand up from your seat, wave, and say: "Yes, sir!" / "Yes, ma’am!".
AE – At Ease - Sit back down and return to your task.
FTF – Fill The Front – Go to the front desks to recruit and let in existing members.
FTB – Fill The Back - Go to the sofas at the back of the room.
BTB – Back To Base – Return to NSC HQ when instructed.
BTW – Back To Work - Resume your assigned task or role.
CTH – Clear The Halls – Go to a station or FTB; don’t crowd the hallway.
Please note: You may not use these commands until you reach the HR division or higher,
but you are still expected to follow them when instructed.

We will now cover the pay rules for NSC.
1. Pay times are at 2 AM, 6 AM, 10 AM, 2 PM, 6 PM, 10 PM GMT+8
2. You MUST have min 30 minutes of online time (OT) in order to claim pay.
3. You MUST fill stations for at least 15 minutes before pay time.
If pay is at 2 PM, fill stations on or before 1:45 and have min 30 mins OT to claim.
4. If you have missed the pay requirements, you will be asked to wait for the next one
5. For every pay session you participate in(provided you meet the eligibility requirements)
the Payer on duty will record your points in the NSC Portal.
6. After advancing to the Security Division(one level above the Standards Division)
you'll be able to redeem credits in the Points Store through the NSC Portal.
If you disconnect during, after or before pay, they are required to immediately notify the
the payer, the member needs to log the disconnection on Discord’s #disconnection-logs with
proper format and tag the payer and any witnesses for vouch.
