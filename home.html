Our Mission

Welcome to the National Security Council, a place where everyone is welcome and everyone is heard. We believe in creating a friendly, respectful and healthy environment where people from all walks of life can come together to learn, grow and collaborate.
As a community rooted in Filipino values, we embrace the spirit of bayanihan, resilience, and hospitality. Through meaningful discussions, cultural exchanges, and engaging activities, we aim to share the rich traditions, history, and diversity of the Philippines with all members.
Join us as we build a safe, inclusive, and empowering space, where your voice matters and where you can connect with like-minded individuals while discovering the beauty of Filipino Culture.
