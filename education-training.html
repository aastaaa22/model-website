Education Training Manual


Welcome to Education Training!
During this training you will learn how to host training lessons, manage trainees, and use the Education tools.
Please be patient and respectful to trainees and other staff.

Introduce yourself: "Hello I am [name], I will be your trainer today"
Explain the training format and the objectives.
Make sure trainees understand how assessments will be done and what is expected.

Keep the class interactive: ask questions, demonstrate examples, and give constructive feedback.
If trainees are silent, prompt them with small tasks or ask them to demonstrate.

Ensure safety and compliance with community rules at all times.
At the end of the session, give a brief recap and allow time for questions.

Assessment examples: practical demonstration, Q&A, or a mini-quiz.
Once trainees pass, update their status in the training portal.
